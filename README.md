## Projects

### 01. Olist E-Commerce Analysis 🛒
**Tools:** PostgreSQL, Python (Pandas), Excel  
**Dataset:** Brazilian E-Commerce (Olist) — 100K+ orders

**Key Analyses:**
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top 10 Product Categories by Revenue
- Order Status Breakdown
- Average Delivery Time
- Top 10 Sellers by Revenue
- Customer Distribution by State
- Payment Type Analysis
- Review Score Distribution

**Key Findings:**
- 24x revenue growth from Oct 2016 to Apr 2018
- Bed & Bath category generated highest revenue (1.69M BRL)
- November 2017 showed biggest spike due to Black Friday
